# Aloe-Vera
A 1.21.1 NeoForge modpack that's all about nature, role-playing, and above all else, building.

This pack was made with easy-going playthroughs and moderate performance in mind. Walk along the beach at night to listen to the sound of the crashing waves, build small cities and settlements with friends with a plethora of building tools and palettes, sleep under a peaceful sky, build complex machines and assembly lines, or fight monsters and bosses at your discretion; a perfect pack for peaceful or hardcore players alike. **Recommended to play WITHOUT SHADERS and with 8GB of allocated memory.**

# Modpack Mechanics
**Hearts and Hunger**
- **Spice of Life: Carrot Edition** makes it so that you're extremely vulnerable in the early game, and for good reason! Explore and experiment with the world for more foods, dishes, and drinks. Become stronger as you do so.

**Apathetic Mobs**
- With **Apathy** installed, if the difficulty is on Easy, (most) vanilla monsters will not attack, making night walks possible and peaceful. Although, still be wary of caving, especially at night...
  - If you want to turn this feature off, change the difficulty to Normal or Hard! 

**Easier building**
- Most of my burnout that comes with Minecraft is the grind for materials and the tedium of procuring large amounts of stone, wood, etc.
  - With **FTB Ultimine** and **Effortless Building**, placing down and spitballing ideas becomes much more streamlined. Build effortlessly to your heart's content!

**Stargazing**
- Take out your spyglass and start gazing! The night sky becomes alive with planets and constellations with the help of **Astrocraft**. Light a campfire, get a sleeping bag, and sleep under a beautiful sky.
  - Maybe you might see an object flying through the sky... or maybe _objects..._

**Easy Multiplayer**
- The modpack features e4mc, which gives users the ability to let their friends connect to their world through the internet, without the use of additional software or mods on the friend side.
- The feature is (only!) activated by the usual means of opening a world to LAN, so the world can be connected both through LAN and from Internet.
